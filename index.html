My Journey So Far...

I have always been a creative individual from a young age. I went to Mesa Community College to pursue a career in art. I knew technology would be the future so I wanted to take my creativity from paper to the screen. I graduated during the beginning of the Covid pandemic and was laid off from my part time job. I found immediate work through Amazon and fell in love with the company and the opportunities it gave me that I otherwise would not have. One of them currently being my education through correlation-1. When I was studying for digital illustration I had always been curious about building websites. Unfortunately my college didn't dive much into it unless I changed career paths. However, correlation-1 has given me the opportunity to explore web development more and combine my previous design skills with it.
